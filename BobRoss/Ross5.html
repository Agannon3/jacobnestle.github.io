Bob Ross

VS. Bill Alexander

Here you are able to see the similarities between Bill Ross and his mentor Bill Alexander.
Controversy stirred when In a 1991 interview with The New York Times, Alexander said of Ross,
"He betrayed me. I invented 'wet on wet'. I trained him, and he is copying me -- what bothers me
is not just that he betrayed me, but that he thinks he can do it better."
