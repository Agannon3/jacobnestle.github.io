Bob Ross

Early Life

Ross was raised in Orlando, Florida

Served in the U.S. Air Force for 20 years (1961 - 1981)

Was a medical records technician and served as a drill
sergeant - “Bust Em Up Bobby”

Took first painting class at a U.S.O. Club in the early 1960s.

Post-military, Ross took private painting classes from
television painter Bill Alexander
