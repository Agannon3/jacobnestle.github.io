San Diego


Andrew Cunanan was born in San Diego, California in 1969 to a Filipino father and

Italian-American mother. Andrew’s family came from modest beginnings but when Andrew’s

father Modesto got a job at Merrill Lynch, a lot changed for the Cunanan family. They moved

into a larger house in a more affluent neighborhood and Modesto bought Andrew a

Mercedes...when he was fourteen. Andrew was admitted to the Bishop’s school after an IQ test

showed an IQ of 147. Andrew was known to tell fantastical lies to his friends at Bishop's, often

saying he was the son of extremely wealthy people or royal families. In his late high school years,

Cunanan had liaisons with older, wealthy gay men. Cunanan often claimed he was friends with

celebrities, specifically Versace. Cunanan attended UC San Diego for a year but soon quit. After

this, he lived with his mother in a small apartment in San Diego and worked at a pharmacy. He

soon began a relationship with Norman Blanchford. Norman was a multi-millionaire businessman who

recently lost his longtime partner of twenty-five years to AIDS.

Before Blanchford, Cunanan did not have access to the high

class lifestyle. Blanchford gave Cunanan 2,500 a month, a 9000 square

foot La Jolla house, and a new car. The two lived together in this house

and enjoyed going to Operas and artistic events. They took a vacation to

Europe where they went to the French Riviera and Venice. This was a

glamorous lifestyle for Cunanan that did not end well. Cunanan demanded

Blanchford give him free first class international and domestic flights, a

Mercedes SL 600, and an increased allowance or else he would leave.

Blanchford declined the ultimatum and Cunanan’s lifestyle began to

deeply regress.
