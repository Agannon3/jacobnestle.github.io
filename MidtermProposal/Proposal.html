Midterm Proposal

My project will be a interactive net art piece, kind of like a fake game through html/css/js?. Upon entering the site, the visitor will be prompted by two buttons asking them where they want to "fish". The buttons will lead them to either a drawn by me or actual photo of a pond and ocean. Upon clicking on certain areas in the bodies of water, the visitor will be taken to a new page, showing them the fish they caught. The fish catch screen will be designed using a grid.
